## Church AI Guiding Principles

> 예수 그리스도 후기성도 교회가 2024년 3월 13일에 도입한 인공지능 사용 가이드 원칙. 7개 원칙이 4개 영역(Spiritual Connection, Transparency, Privacy and Security, Accountability)으로 묶인다. 공식 페이지: `AI.ChurchofJesusChrist.org`. *General Handbook* §38.8.48이 이를 정책으로 codify.

## 도입

- **공식 발표일**: 2024년 3월 13일 수요일, 전 세계 교회 직원 대상 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
- **공동 발표자**:
  - Elder Gerrit W. Gong of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles (Church Communication Committee 공동 의장)
  - Elder John C. Pingree of the Seventy (Correlation Department 사무 책임자)
- Elder Gong의 도입 슬로건: "We can create appropriate balances in our perceptions, plans, and implementation of generative AI that are realistic both of opportunity and challenge — put another way, that are neither giddy nor alarmist" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
- Elder Pingree의 적용 범위 정리: "The guiding principles are intended to support the responsible use of AI by the Church workforce. These principles may also be helpful for members and friends of the Church as they navigate a world changing due to AI" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].

## 7개 원칙 / 4개 영역

> 본 섹션의 모든 원칙 문구는 2024-03-13 발표 원문 그대로 인용. 7-원칙/4-영역 카운트 정식화는 Istanbul footnote xi에서 확인 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md].

### Spiritual Connection

> The Church will use artificial intelligence to support and not supplant connection between God and His children.

- The Church will use artificial intelligence in positive, helpful, and uplifting ways that maintain the honesty, integrity, ethics, values, and standards of the Church.

### Transparency

- People interacting with the Church will understand when they are interfacing with artificial intelligence.
- The Church will provide attribution for content created with artificial intelligence when the authenticity, accuracy, or authorship of the content could be misunderstood or misleading.

### Privacy and Security

- The Church's use of artificial intelligence will safeguard sacred and personal information.

### Accountability

- The Church will use artificial intelligence in a manner consistent with the policies of the Church and all applicable laws.
- The Church will be measured and deliberate in its use of artificial intelligence by regularly testing and reviewing outputs to help ensure accuracy, truthfulness, and compliance.

> **Synthesis** (from: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md): Elder Gong은 Istanbul footnote xi에서 "seven 'Principles for Church Use of Artificial Intelligence' in the areas of Spiritual Connection, Transparency, Privacy and Security, and Accountability"라고 정확히 표현. 7개 원칙은 Spiritual Connection 헤더 문장(1) + sub-bullet(1) + Transparency 2 + Privacy and Security 1 + Accountability 2 = 7로 카운트된다.

## 정책 차원 — *General Handbook* §38.8.48

*General Handbook: Serving in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints* **§38.8.48** "Appropriate Use of Artificial Intelligence"가 가이드라인을 운영 정책으로 codify. 본 섹션 모든 명제는 같은 출처 [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-handbook-enduring-guidance-ai.md]:

- **일반 원칙**: AI는 "individual effort or divine inspiration required for personal spiritual growth or genuine relationships with God and others"를 대체할 수 없음 [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md].
- **금지**: 회원은 AI를 거짓·오도·불법·해로운 컨텐츠 생성·배포에 사용하면 안 됨. AI 생성 결과의 정확성을 검증해야 함 [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md].
- **§38.8.48.1 Learning and Teaching**: "AI cannot replace the gift of divine inspiration or the individual work required to receive it." 가르침은 경전·살아 있는 선지자·성신의 가이드로 ([[divine-learning-pattern]] 참조) [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md] (§38.8.48.1).
- **§38.8.48.2 Relationships with God and Others**: AI는 하나님·타인과의 의미 있는 관계를 대체할 수 없음 — [[covenant-belonging|Thou·They·It·I]] 관계의 정책적 응답 [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md] (§38.8.48.2).
- **§38.8.48.3 Callings and Assignments** — 4개 구체 원칙 [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md] (§38.8.48.3):
  - 사용 시 교회 integrity·standards·teachings를 옹호하는 방식으로
  - 신적 영감이 필요한 말씀·교훈·기도·축복 준비는 AI로 대체할 수 없으나, research·editing·translation 등에는 helpful
  - 지도자는 의료·재정·법률·기타 민감 사안에 대해 AI에 자문을 의지하지 말 것 (필요시 전문가, §31.3.6 참조)
  - 교회 기록·회원 개인 데이터·기밀 통신 등 민감 정보는 교회가 제공·관리하지 않는 AI에 입력 금지
- **공식 참조 URL**: 핸드북 본문 마지막 줄이 회원·지도자에게 추가 정보를 위해 **ChurchofJesusChrist.org/AI**를 참조하도록 안내 — [[church-ai-guiding-principles|Church AI 가이드라인]] 7-원칙/4-영역의 공식 landing page (`sources/articles/2026-05-18-church-ai-learn-page.md`와 동일) [source: sources/notes/2026-05-18-general-handbook-38-8-48-ai-v2.md].

## 직원·자원봉사자 대상 보조 지침 (Newsroom)

Elder Gong이 발표에서 강조한 두 가지 추가 주의 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md]:

1. AI의 속도·간편함을 빌미로 회원을 오디오·영상 컨텐츠로 oversaturate하지 말 것.
2. 회복된 교회는 "primarily a purveyor of information"이 아니라 "*a source of God's truth*"이다 — AI를 정보 vendor처럼 다루지 말 것.
