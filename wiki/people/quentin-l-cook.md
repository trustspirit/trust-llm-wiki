# Elder Quentin L. Cook

> 예수 그리스도 후기성도 교회 십이사도 정원회(Quorum of the Twelve Apostles) 구성원. Stanford Law School 졸업(1966), Silicon Valley에서 변호사로 활동. 2026년 3월 BYU 150주년 devotional "Faith in Jesus Christ in the Information Age"에서 농경시대 → 산업시대 → 정보시대 → AI시대로의 전환을 prophets의 가르침과 함께 분석.

## 약력 (Cook 본인 회고)

- Stanford Law School 졸업(1966), Silicon Valley에서 법률 실무 경험 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- 1977년에 본인 법률사무소가 client의 hard drive를 Apple Computer에 매각/합병하는 협상에 참여 — Steve Jobs (당시 22세), Steve Wozniak (당시 27세)와 직접 만남 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- 1986년 George P. Shultz의 "The Shape, Scope, and Consequences of the Age of Information" 연설에 직접 참석 — 본인이 산타 프란시스코에서 들음 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- 1840년 Quorum of the Twelve Apostles의 영국 여정을 본인 부인 Mary와 함께 재현한 여행기 (2025년 여름) — Cheltenham → Paddington Station을 동일 경로로 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

## "Faith in Jesus Christ in the Information Age" (2026-03-03 BYU Devotional)

### 핵심 프레임: 4개 시대 전환

세계가 지난 150년 동안 거친 네 시대 (BYU 150주년 기념 강연 맥락) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md]:

1. **농경시대 (Agricultural Age)** — 가족이 세대를 거쳐 함께 일함; 지식은 경험·best practices로; 삶이 계절에 맞춰짐
2. **산업시대 (Industrial Age)** — 1869년 미국 횡단철도가 utah에서 완성 → 농경에서 산업으로의 주요 전환; 도시·표준화된 교육; 가족 간 세대 연결 약화
3. **정보시대 (Information Age)** — Shultz가 1986년 정의한 "information revolution"; 컴퓨터·인터넷
4. **인공지능시대 (Artificial Intelligence Age)** — 정보시대가 "morphing into" AI 시대로 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md]

이 전환은 우연이 아님: "We live in the dispensation of the fulness of times, a period when knowledge has increased rapidly and the Lord's work is being hastened and accelerated across the earth" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

### AI 시대를 형성할 세 요소

- artificial intelligence
- algorithms that can shape attention and belief
- automation of both physical and cognitive labor

[source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md]

### 핵심 명제 — "Choose Truth When Deception Is Easy"

- 슬로건: "in the artificial intelligence age, you need to choose truth when deception is easy" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- "Slow down enough to listen to the Spirit and to allow Him to direct you. We must all learn to use **technology as a servant, not as a master**" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- "The future of the Church and of our very civilization depends on members and individuals who have deep faith, moral courage, and the ability to navigate an increasingly complex world" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

### Two Methods of Gaining Knowledge (Cook이 인용한 [[dallin-h-oaks|President Oaks]] 명제)

President Dallin H. Oaks의 2026-02-10 BYU devotional "Coming Closer to Jesus Christ"에서: "We are grateful to know that there are two methods of gaining needed knowledge: (1) the evolving disclosures of man discovered by the **scientific method** and (2) the truths disclosed by the **spiritual method**, which begins with faith in God and relies on scriptures, inspired teaching, and personal revelation. There is no ultimate conflict between knowledge gained by these different methods because God, our omnipotent Eternal Father, knows all truth and beckons us to learn by both methods" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

### 기술의 양면성 사례

- **긍정**: 선교 사업·성전 사역에 AI 기술 도움 — "almost exponential" 성장 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- **부정**: 초기 영화 산업이 알코올·담배를 "정상적·매력적·세련된" 방식으로 정상화 → President Heber J. Grant이 [[divine-learning-pattern|선지자적 가이드]]로 Word of Wisdom 강조 → 90년 뒤 의료계가 같은 결론에 도달 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- "spiritual guidance can help you overcome deception as you consume information old or new" — 선지자적 가이드의 시간차 우위 사례 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
