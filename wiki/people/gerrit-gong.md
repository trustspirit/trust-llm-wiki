# Elder Gerrit W. Gong

> 예수 그리스도 후기성도 교회 십이사도 정원회(Quorum of the Twelve Apostles) 구성원. 2024–2025년에 교회 내부 [[church-ai-guiding-principles|AI 가이드라인]] 도입과 외부 3대 무대(Istanbul Religions for Peace, BYU Education Week, Rome Summit on Ethics and AI)에서의 강연을 통해 [[faith-and-ethics-ai-evaluation]] 구상을 단계적으로 공개·발전시킴.

## 약력 (Istanbul footnote ii)

- 학력 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md]:
  - PhD, MA in International Relations — Oxford University, Rhodes Scholar
  - BA in Asian and University Studies, *summa cum laude* — Brigham Young University
- 경력 (전임 교회 봉사 전 워싱턴 D.C. 20년 + BYU 10년) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md]:
  - U.S. Department of State: Special Assistant to the Undersecretary of State; Special Assistant to two U.S. Ambassadors in Beijing, PRC; Policy Planning Staff consultant
  - Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS): Asia Director and China Chair
  - Brigham Young University, Provo: Assistant to the President for Planning and Assessment (10년)
  - U.S. Secretary of Education's National Advisory Committee for Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI) 위원
- 가족 배경 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md]:
  - 부모는 Stanford University에서 만남; 본인은 Silicon Valley에서 성장
  - 부친 Professor Walter A. Gong은 트랜지스터 공동 발명으로 노벨상을 수상한 William Shockley와 함께 물리학 교재 *Mechanics* 공저
  - State Department 시절 George Shultz 국무장관이 "정보화 시대 외교의 형태·범위·결과"를 함께 모색하는 업무를 맡김 (Shultz, *The Shape, Scope, and Consequences of the Age of Information*, 1986)
- 자칭 "international name": Gerrit (네덜란드), Walter (미국, 부친 이름), Gong (중국 성) — "household of faith 안의 global citizen" 정체성 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md].

## AI에 관한 핵심 입장

- 균형 슬로건: "AI is neither the sum of, nor the solution to, all our opportunities or problems" — AI를 두려워하지도, 만능으로 여기지도 않음 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md].
- 도입 슬로건 (Newsroom 2024-03-13): "We can create appropriate balances in our perceptions, plans, and implementation of generative AI that are realistic both of opportunity and challenge — put another way, that are neither giddy nor alarmist" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
- BYU 2025-08 재정식화: "We are not afraid of artificial intelligence, nor do we think it is the answer to every problem. As we work hard and smart, AI can be a valuable tool to enhance but not replace our own efforts" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-an-ear-to-wisdom-a-heart-to-understanding.md].
- 규제 스펙트럼에서 "의미 있는 규제 없음 ↔ 숨막히는 과잉 규제" 두 극단 사이의 균형 노선 지지 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md].
- "Profit-driven technology companies should not be determining society's AI moral compass" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md].
- 신학적 단정: "God is God. AI is not and cannot be God" — [[agi]]/ASI 달성 여부와 무관하게 신은 신, AI는 AI [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-an-ear-to-wisdom-a-heart-to-understanding.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md].
- Istanbul 추가 논거: AI 훈련 데이터는 사람이 소싱한 것이고 AI 도덕·윤리 원칙은 만든 자가 의식적으로 주입·정렬한 것일 뿐. AGI/ASI에 도달해도 "reasoning — even superhuman reasoning — has limitations" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md].
- AI 챗봇 정서적 의존에 대한 경구: "To sit alone with an AI chatbot is really to sit alone. It is soliloquy masquerading as dialogue" — Solace = Soul-less 비유 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md].
- 회원의 영적 일에 대한 경계: "we will not grow spiritually if we let artificial intelligence write our sacrament meeting talks or do our seminary homework" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-an-ear-to-wisdom-a-heart-to-understanding.md].
- 직원 대상 두 가지 주의 (Newsroom 2024-03-13): (1) AI의 속도·간편함을 빌미로 회원을 컨텐츠로 oversaturate 금지 (2) 교회는 정보 vendor가 아니라 "*a source of God's truth*" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].

## [[deepfakes]] 분석 (Newsroom 2024-03-13)

- 정의: "the fictitious or malicious use of someone's name, image, and likeness" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
- 가장 표적 받는 인물 3중 조건: 인지도 있는 얼굴·음성 + 공개 영상/음성 자료 + 알려진 동기 있는 적대자 → 교회 지도자가 그 프로파일 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
- 면역 자원 3가지: 성신(Holy Spirit), 지혜(Wisdom), 신뢰할 만한 출처(Trusted Sources) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-newsroom-church-ai-guiding-principles.md].
