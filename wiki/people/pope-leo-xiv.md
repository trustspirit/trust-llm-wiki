## AI에 관한 핵심 입장

### AI는 도구, 의도가 윤리적 무게를 결정 (2025-06-17)

- Pope Francis의 G7 (2024-06-14) 진술을 직접 계승 인용: "AI is *above all else a tool*" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].
- "tools point to the human intelligence that crafted them and draw much of their ethical force from the intentions of the individuals that wield them" — 도구의 윤리적 힘은 사용자 의도에서 나옴 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].
- 평가 기준: "the well-being of the human person not only materially, but also intellectually and spiritually" — 인간의 영적·지적 안녕까지 고려 (*Antiqua et Nova*, 6 인용) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].

### 청년·지능·진정한 지혜 (2025-06-17)

- 청년 발달에 대한 우려: "we are all concerned for children and young people, and the possible consequences of the use of AI on their intellectual and neurological development" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].
- "access to data — however extensive — must not be confused with intelligence, which necessarily 'involves the person's openness to the ultimate questions of life and reflects an orientation toward the True and the Good'" (*Antiqua et Nova*, 29 인용) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].
- "authentic wisdom has more to do with recognizing the true meaning of life, than with the availability of data" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md].

### AI 개발자의 도덕적 의무 (2025-11-03 Builders AI Forum)

- "AI, like all human invention, springs from the creative capacity that God has entrusted to us" (*Antiqua et Nova*, 37 인용) — 기술 혁신은 신적 창조 행위 참여의 한 형태 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md].
- 결정적 명제: "**every design choice expresses a vision of humanity**" — 설계 결정은 인간관의 표현이며 윤리적·영적 무게를 갖는다 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md].
- "The Church therefore calls all builders of AI to cultivate **moral discernment as a fundamental part of their work** — to develop systems that reflect justice, solidarity, and a genuine reverence for life" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md].
- "intelligence — whether artificial or human — finds its fullest meaning in **love, freedom and relationship with God**" — "dialogue between faith and reason"의 디지털 시대 재정식화 (*Antiqua et Nova*, 83 인용) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md].

### AI와 의료, 인간 존엄 (2025-11-07)

- "ontological dignity that belongs to the person as such simply because he or she exists and is willed, created, and loved by God" (*Dignitas Infinita*, 7 인용) — 인간의 존재론적 존엄은 능력·기여와 무관 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].
- "healthcare professionals have the vocation and responsibility to be guardians and servants of human life" especially in vulnerable stages (*Antiqua et Nova*, 71 인용) — 의료 AI 운영자에게 동일 적용 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].
- "the greater the fragility of human life, the greater the nobility required of those entrusted with its care" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].
- 의료 본질 재정식화: "Medical professionalism … requires not only the necessary specific expertise, but also the ability to communicate and be close to others. It can never be reduced merely to solving a problem" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].
- AI 사용 조건: "if AI is to serve human dignity and the effective provision of healthcare, we must ensure that it truly enhances both interpersonal relationships and the care provided" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].
- 디지털 혁명이 "epochal change" (Pope Francis 용어): "We currently interact with machines as if they were interlocutors, and thus become almost an extension of them. In this sense, we not only run the risk of losing sight of the faces of the people around us, but of forgetting how to recognize and cherish all that is truly human" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].

- 세 메시지 모두 *Antiqua et Nova*를 명시적으로 인용 — 후속 교황의 해석·적용으로 기능 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md].

> **Synthesis** (from: sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-ethics-corporate-governance.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-builders-ai-forum.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-leo-xiv-ai-medicine-human-dignity.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-antiqua-et-nova-vatican.md): Pope Leo XIV의 세 메시지는 *Antiqua et Nova*의 abstract 신학 원리를 세 산업 영역(기업 거버넌스 → 개발자 윤리 → 의료)에 단계적으로 적용한다.
