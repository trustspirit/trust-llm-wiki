## President Dallin H. Oaks

> 예수 그리스도 후기성도 교회 **18대 회장** (set apart 2025-10-14, [[russell-m-nelson|President Russell M. Nelson]] 별세 후 승계). 이전 BYU 학생·BYU 회장 역임. Nelson의 First Counselor in the First Presidency였다 회장직 승계. AI 시대 [[divine-learning-pattern|진리 분별]]의 핵심 명제 "Two Methods of Gaining Knowledge"(scientific + spiritual)와 "becoming" 신학의 권위 source.

## 회장 승계 (2025)

- 2025년 9월 27일 President Russell M. Nelson 별세 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-funeral.md].
- 별세 직후(2025-09-29): Oaks가 Quorum of the Twelve President로서 공식 statement 발표 — Nelson 별세와 2025-09-28 Grand Blanc, Michigan 폭력 사건(교회 회원 대상)을 함께 다룸 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-grand-blanc-statement.md].
- October 2025 General Conference (Nelson 별세 며칠 후 개최): Oaks가 Quorum of the Twelve President 자격으로 사회. 그는 본인이 "이미 7년 반 동안 ward, stake, general conference에서 sustained"되어 왔음을 확인 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].
- 18대 회장으로 set apart: **2025-10-14** [source: WebSearch 결과 2026-05-18, 1차 source 미수집 — 향후 set-apart 공식 발표 ingest 후보]. Nelson 별세 후 First Presidency 재조직의 표준 패턴.

## 약력 (확인된 사항)

- 2018년 1월 14일 [[russell-m-nelson|President Russell M. Nelson]]에 의해 First Counselor in the First Presidency로 임명 — Nelson이 12명 사도 1대1 면담 후 성전 private room에서 주님의 뜻을 구해 선택 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 회장 임명 이전 7년 반 동안 Quorum of the Twelve President 직책을 동시에 보유 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].
- BYU 회장 역임 (이전 BYU 학생) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- 2026-01에 Burley Idaho Temple 헌납 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

## 핵심 가르침

### "Two Methods of Gaining Knowledge" (BYU 2026-02-10)

"Coming Closer to Jesus Christ" devotional의 결정적 명제 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-coming-closer-to-jesus-christ.md]:

> We are grateful to know that there are two methods of gaining needed knowledge: (1) the evolving disclosures of man discovered by the **scientific method** and (2) the truths disclosed by the **spiritual method**, which begins with faith in God and relies on scriptures, inspired teaching, and personal revelation. There is no ultimate conflict between knowledge gained by these different methods because God, our omnipotent Eternal Father, knows all truth and beckons us to learn by both methods.

→ AI 시대 진리 분별([[divine-learning-pattern]], [[deepfakes]])의 권위 명제 — 과학적 방법과 영적 방법은 충돌하지 않으며 둘 다 필요.

추가 명제: 과학·신앙 사이 외견상 불일치에 대한 권고는 Elder Richard L. Evans 인용 (*CR* April 1961, 76): "There may be some seeming discrepancies. Do not worry about them. Eternity is a long time. … science after all (even when it is true and final and factual) is simply man's discovering of a few things that God already knows and controls in his ordering of the universe. … **God has not told us all he knows**. We believe in continuous revelation. Be patient. Keep humble and balanced in all things" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-coming-closer-to-jesus-christ.md].

### "Becoming" 신학 ("The Challenge to Become", Ensign November 2000)

본인이 십이사도 정원회 구성원이었을 때 General Conference 강연 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenge-to-become.md]:

> The Final Judgment is not just an evaluation of a sum total of good and evil acts. … It is an acknowledgment of the final effect of our acts and thoughts—what we have **become**.

→ [[quentin-l-cook|Elder Cook]]이 2026-03-03 강연에서 "President Oaks is an almost perfect example of something he has taught for many years: striving to become what we ought to become"라고 평가 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].

### Grand Blanc Statement (2025-09-29)

별세하신 Nelson에 대한 추모와 2025-09-28 Grand Blanc 비극을 함께 다룬 statement의 핵심 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-grand-blanc-statement.md]:

- "Our hearts are heavy with sorrow. Millions of us are mourning the passing of our beloved Prophet and President, Russell M. Nelson. He was a dear friend and a cherished leader. His timeless teachings continue to guide us and help us find comfort amid suffering"
- "The awful tragedy that took place in Grand Blanc, Michigan, on September 28, reminds us of our sacred responsibilities as followers of Jesus Christ. We mourn with our members who have lost loved ones"
- "May we all remember the truth that **each of us is a beloved child of God**. Our Savior Jesus Christ, through His infinite Atonement, overcame death and gives us the joyful assurance that each of us will be gloriously resurrected"

### October 2025 General Conference Introduction

회장 승계 직후 강연의 핵심 명제 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md]:

- Nelson의 사망 후에도 General Conference는 "speakers and music assigned by President Nelson after months of preparation" 그대로 진행 — Nelson의 영적 권위가 일정·내용을 거기까지 미친다.
- "President Nelson understood the value of general conference to provide direction to the Saints in the coming months. We honor him by following the planned conference schedule he approved."
- Oaks의 self-evaluation: "I loved Russell M. Nelson and have learned more about the gospel and gospel leadership from my long friendship and association with him than from any other leader I have personally known. He is our model as a servant and follower of the Lord Jesus Christ."

### BYU 비전과 "secular learning이 horizons를 제한하지 않게"

"Coming Closer to Jesus Christ"에서 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-coming-closer-to-jesus-christ.md]:

- BYU vision (원전 1차 source): BYU "will become the great university of the Lord—not in the world's way but in the Lord's way" — Oaks "Challenges to the Mission of Brigham Young University" (BYU Leadership Conference, 2017-04-21), Cook이 2026-03 강연에서 재인용 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenges-mission-byu.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-cook-faith-jesus-christ-information-age.md].
- 보조 명제: "To accomplish its mission, BYU must have all parts of its community united in pursuing it" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenges-mission-byu.md].
- 통념 거부: 일부 학계 인사가 "you can either be secular or second-rate"라고 종교 affiliated 대학에 양자택일을 강요하지만, Oaks는 이를 거부 — "this is not a lost cause by any stretch of the imagination" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenges-mission-byu.md].
- David Brooks (Jewish NYT 칼럼니스트) 인용: Yale 학생들이 "so hungry for spiritual knowledge" — 세속 envrionment에서도 영적 갈증이 있다는 외부 증언 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenges-mission-byu.md].
- 회원 책임: "BYU faculty to offer public, unassigned support of Church policies that others were challenging on secular grounds" — "Note that word unassigned. The Church should not have to ask or assign. The duty is inherent in the position" — Oaks "It Hasn't Been Easy and It Won't Get Easier" (BYU leadership conference, 2014-08-25)에서 본인 발언을 2017에서 재인용. **원문은 본 위키에 미수집** (BYU leadership conference는 일반 공개 X — 향후 ingest 후보) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-challenges-mission-byu.md].
- 본인 권고: "Keep the commandments and be true to the covenants … to guide you along the covenant path. **Never let your secular learning limit your horizons**" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-coming-closer-to-jesus-christ.md].
- 신뢰 우려: "Those of diminishing faith and activity in the restored Church are a major source of concern to your prophet leaders" — 회장 이전부터의 prophetic 우려 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-coming-closer-to-jesus-christ.md].
