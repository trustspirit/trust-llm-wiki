# President Russell M. Nelson (1924–2025)

> 예수 그리스도 후기성도 교회의 **17대 회장** (재임 2018-01-14 ~ 2025-09-27). 십이사도 정원회에서 34년 봉사 후 회장직 시작. 의사(외과의) 출신. 2025년 9월 27일 101세로 별세. 후임은 [[dallin-h-oaks|President Dallin H. Oaks]] (18대 회장). 2018년 4월 첫 총회 강연 "Revelation for the Church, Revelation for Our Lives"가 본 위키 AI 클러스터에서 [[divine-learning-pattern|개인 계시 절차]]의 1차 권위 source.

## 별세와 장례 (2025)

- **별세**: 2025년 9월 27일, 101세 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-funeral.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-grand-blanc-statement.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].
- 별세 다음 날(2025-09-28) Grand Blanc, Michigan에서 교회 회원 대상 폭력 사건 발생 — Oaks가 2025-09-29 공식 statement로 두 비극을 함께 다룸 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-grand-blanc-statement.md].
- **장례식**: Conference Center on Temple Square. 4명의 교회 지도자 + 그의 자녀 2명이 추도사 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-funeral.md].
- President Dallin H. Oaks (당시 Quorum of the Twelve President) 추도 발언: "His writings and his influence and his example live on. And so does our grief at his passing. … [His life] bore witness of his committed testimony as a prophet and servant of the Lord Jesus Christ" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-funeral.md].
- Oaks의 회고: "I loved Russell M. Nelson and have learned more about the gospel and gospel leadership from my long friendship and association with him than from any other leader I have personally known. He is our model as a servant and follower of the Lord Jesus Christ" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].
- October 2025 General Conference (별세 며칠 후 개최)는 Nelson이 본인 사망 전 몇 달 동안 준비·승인한 일정·발언자·음악대로 진행됨 — Oaks의 "Introduction"과 Sunday afternoon session에서 Nelson을 대신한 강연만 추가 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].
- "약 75년 만에" Church President가 General Conference 며칠 전에 별세한 사례 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-oaks-introduction-october-2025.md].

## 배경

- 의대 재학 중인 1945년 Salt Lake Temple에서 Dantzel White와 결혼; 슬하 9녀 1남 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 60년 결혼 후 2005년 Dantzel 별세, 2006년 4월 6일 Salt Lake Temple에서 Wendy Watson과 재혼 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 십이사도 정원회에서 34년 봉사. 이전 회장 16명 중 10명을 개인적으로 알고 있었음 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 8명의 증조부모 모두 유럽에서 교회로 개종, Zion으로 이주. 본인은 복음 중심 가정에서 자라지 않음 — 어린 시절 LDS 서점에서 교회 책을 찾아 읽음 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].

- 2018년 1월 14일 Salt Lake Temple 상실에서 First Presidency 재조직 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 보좌 선택 과정: 12명 사도와 개별 1대1 면담 후 성전 private room에서 주님의 뜻을 구해 **President Dallin H. Oaks**와 **President Henry B. Eyring**을 보좌로 지명 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- 같은 4월 총회에서 **Elder Gerrit W. Gong**과 **Elder Ulisses Soares**의 사도 임명 발표 — 본 위키 [[gerrit-gong]] 사도 봉사 시작의 정전적 source [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].

## "Revelation for the Church, Revelation for Our Lives" (2018-04-01)

부활 주일 General Conference 강연 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md]. 본 위키 AI 클러스터에서 다수 인용되는 권위 source.

### 핵심 명제

- **"The privilege of receiving revelation is one of the greatest gifts of God to His children"** — Elder Gong이 Istanbul (2025-07)에서 신과의 직접 교제의 우선성 논증에 인용한 바로 그 문구 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md].
- **"In coming days, it will not be possible to survive spiritually without the guiding, directing, comforting, and constant influence of the Holy Ghost"** — Elder Bednar가 "Things as They Really Are 2.0" (2024-11) 결말부에서 인용 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-bednar-things-as-they-really-are-2-0.md].
- "Nothing opens the heavens quite like the combination of *increased purity, exact obedience, earnest seeking, daily feasting on the words of Christ in the Book of Mormon, and regular time committed to temple and family history work*" — 천국이 열리는 5요소 조합 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].
- "If we are to have any hope of sifting through the myriad of voices and the philosophies of men that attack truth, we must learn to receive revelation" — 진리 분별의 핵심 능력으로 personal revelation 제시 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].

### 개인 계시의 실용 절차

President Nelson의 권고 절차 — Joseph Smith의 Sacred Grove 사례를 본받아 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md]:

1. **Find a quiet place** where you can regularly go
2. **Humble yourself** before God
3. **Pour out your heart** to your Heavenly Father about concerns, fears, weaknesses
4. **Listen** — write the thoughts that come to mind, record feelings
5. **Follow through with actions** prompted to take
6. **Repeat** day after day, month after month, year after year → "grow into the principle of revelation"

이 절차는 [[divine-learning-pattern|Elder Barcellos의 4단계 학습 패턴]]과 구조적으로 동일 (search → ponder → ask → confirmed → act).

### 의사로서의 계시 체험

- "I remember in an operating room, I have stood over a patient—unsure how to perform an unprecedented procedure—and experienced the Holy Ghost diagramming the technique in my mind" — 본인의 외과의 시기 개인 계시 체험 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].

### First Presidency / Quorum of the Twelve 운영

- "In our meetings, the majority never rules! We listen prayerfully to one another and talk with each other until we are united" — 만장일치 + 성신의 통일성으로 결정 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md].

## "Think Celestial!" (2023-10-01)

99세 생일 직후 General Conference 강연. AI를 직접 언급하지는 않지만 본 위키 AI 클러스터에서 다수의 명제가 직접 적용 가능 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].

### 핵심 슬로건

- **"Think celestial"** = "being spiritually minded" — *몰몬경* Jacob의 "to be spiritually-minded is life eternal" 인용 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].
- 첫 회장 메시지의 echo: "begin with the end in mind" — 셀레스천 왕국을 영원한 목표로 두고 mortal 결정을 역산 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].
- 적용 상황: "When you are confronted with a dilemma, think celestial! When tested by temptation, think celestial! When life or loved ones let you down, think celestial!" — 일상의 모든 결정 프레임 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].

### Addiction-as-idol 명제 ([[moral-agency]] / [[ai-companions]] 적용 권위)

> "As you think celestial, you will find yourself avoiding *anything* that robs you of your agency. *Any* addiction—be it gaming, gambling, debt, drugs, alcohol, anger, pornography, sex, or even food—offends God. **Why? Because your obsession becomes your god. You look to *it* rather than to *Him* for solace.**"

[source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md]

→ AI 챗봇/companions 의존이 같은 카테고리에 들어갈 수 있는 신학적 권위 근거. "obsession becomes your god" 명제는 [[antiqua-et-nova|*Antiqua et Nova* §V 104-107]]의 "substituting God for an artifact of human making is idolatry"와 직접 평행.

### Deceptions와 personal revelation 강화

- "There is no end to the adversary's deceptions. Please be prepared. Never take counsel from those who do not believe" — [[deepfakes]] 시대 적용 가능 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].
- "Seek guidance from voices you can trust—from prophets, seers, and revelators and from the whisperings of the Holy Ghost" — Elder Gong이 [[deepfakes|딥페이크 면역 자원]]으로 정리한 "trusted sources"의 원전 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].
- "Please do the spiritual work to increase your capacity to receive personal revelation" — [[divine-learning-pattern|개인 계시 절차]]의 강화 명제 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].

### 신규 성전 20곳 발표 (2023-10)

Savai'i (Samoa), Cancún·Piura·Huancayo (Mexico·Peru), Viña del Mar (Chile), Goiânia·João Pessoa (Brazil), Calabar·Cape Coast·Luanda·Mbuji-Mayi (Nigeria·Ghana·Angola·DR Congo), Laoag·Osaka (Philippines·Japan), Kahului·Fairbanks·Vancouver·Colorado Springs·Tulsa·Roanoke (Hawaii·Alaska·WA·CO·OK·VA), Ulaanbaatar (Mongolia) [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].

### 본인 경험 일화

- 99세 생일 직후, 등 근육 부상으로 General Conference 강연을 처음으로 앉아서 진행 — "I have delivered more than 100 general conference addresses standing, today I thought I would do so sitting" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].
- 인턴 시절(월급 $15) tithing 일화: Dantzel의 권유로 $1.50을 추가 납부하기 시작 — "paying tithing is all about faith, not money" [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-think-celestial.md].

## AI 시대 적용 (사도들의 인용 방식)

- President Nelson 본인은 (이 위키에 ingest된 source 안에서는) AI를 직접 논하지 않지만, 그의 "Revelation for the Church, Revelation for Our Lives" 명제들이 AI 시대 신학 응답의 토대 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-nelson-revelation-for-the-church.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-elder-gong-faith-respect-moral-compass-ai.md, sources/articles/2026-05-18-bednar-things-as-they-really-are-2-0.md].
- Istanbul Gong 인용: AI가 신·인간의 직접 교제를 대체할 수 없다는 명제의 권위 근거 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-gong-istanbul-faith-ethics-ai-call-to-action.md].
- Bednar 인용: 성신의 constant influence가 영적 생존에 필수라는 명제의 권위 근거 [source: sources/articles/2026-05-18-bednar-things-as-they-really-are-2-0.md].
